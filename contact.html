Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in UX/UI design for digital products, including web applications, mobile apps, and responsive websites. I'm particularly interested in fintech, health tech, and e-commerce projects.

What is your design process?

My design process typically includes research, ideation, wireframing, prototyping, and testing. I believe in an iterative approach that involves users throughout the process to ensure the final product meets their needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A simple website redesign might take 4-6 weeks, while a complex application could take 3-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline estimate after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you work remotely?

Yes, I work remotely with clients around the world. I use tools like Figma, Slack, and Zoom to collaborate effectively regardless of location.
